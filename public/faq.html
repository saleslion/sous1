Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about your AI cooking assistant

Sousie is an AI-powered cooking assistant that helps you discover and create delicious recipes. Using advanced AI technology, Sousie can generate personalized recipes based on the ingredients you have available, suggest surprise meal combinations, and provide interactive cooking guidance through our chat interface.

Simply tell Sousie what ingredients you have, any dietary preferences, and let our AI create customized recipes with detailed instructions, cooking tips, and even fun culinary anecdotes!

Using the Recipe Finder is easy! Navigate to the "Recipe Finder" section and enter the ingredients you have in your kitchen. For example, type "chicken, rice, onions" in the ingredients field.

You can also add any dietary restrictions or preferences in the optional field (like "vegetarian", "gluten-free", or "low-carb"). Then click "Find Recipes" and Sousie will generate multiple creative meal pairing suggestions with detailed recipes!

The "Surprise Me" feature is perfect when you're feeling adventurous or need culinary inspiration! Instead of specifying ingredients, Sousie will generate completely random, creative recipe combinations.

Each surprise includes unique meal pairings with both main dishes and complementary sides or desserts. It's a great way to discover new cuisines, try different cooking techniques, and break out of your usual cooking routine!

Sousie is completely free to use! You can generate recipes, chat with the AI, and access all core features without any payment or subscription.

While creating an account is optional, it allows you to save your favorite recipes, track your cooking history, and access additional features like weekly menu planning. You can start using Sousie immediately without signing up - just dive in and start cooking!
